Little bit about me!

Hello, I'm Tejaswini, a graduate student pursuing an MS in Robotics with a Computer Science concentration at Northeastern University. My academic journey is driven by a genuine passion for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision.

During my time at Northeastern, I've had the opportunity to work on several exciting projects that have deepened my understanding of robotics and its applications. These projects have not only enhanced my technical skills but also reinforced my problem-solving abilities and creativity in approaching complex challenges. In addition to my coursework, I served as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for the Pattern Recognition and Computer Vision course, where I applied my knowledge of the field.

Prior to my graduate studies, I worked as an ETCS Application Engineer at Alstom, where I contributed to railway signaling projects and gained valuable industry experience. More recently, I completed a co-op at Festo Corporation, working on mechatronics and electrical engineering projects that broadened my practical knowledge.

I'm excited about the potential of robotics to solve real-world problems and am always eager to learn and contribute to this rapidly evolving field. If you're interested in discussing robotics, AI, or potential collaborations, I'd be delighted to connect!
